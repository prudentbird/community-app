# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Local Community Business Connector App

## 1. Introduction/Overview

This document outlines the product requirements for a new community application designed to facilitate connections between individual user profiles and local businesses within a specific city. The primary goal is to foster a vibrant local ecosystem by making it easier for residents to discover and engage with local businesses, and for businesses to connect with their target local audience.

## 2. Goals & Objectives

- **Primary Goal:** To build a robust community platform that strengthens local commerce and engagement.
- **Objective 1:** Increase local business visibility and discovery for city residents.
- **Objective 2:** Provide local businesses with a dedicated platform to showcase their offerings and promotions.
- **Objective 3:** Enable meaningful interactions and connections between users and businesses.
- **Objective 4:** Drive economic activity and community participation within the designated city.

## 3. Target Audience

- **City Residents/Users:** Individuals living in the target city who are interested in exploring local businesses, services, events, and connecting with their community.
- **Local Businesses:** Small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) operating within the target city, including retail, services, hospitality, and more, seeking to reach local customers and build their brand.

## 4. Key Features (High-Level)

- **User Profiles:** Creation and management of personal profiles for residents, including interests and preferences.
- **Business Profiles:** Comprehensive profiles for local businesses, showcasing details like services, products, operating hours, location, contact information, photos, and special offers.
- **Discovery & Search:** Intuitive search and browse functionalities for users to find businesses by category, location, keywords, or recommendations.
- **Connection & Engagement Tools:** Features allowing users to follow businesses, write reviews, rate experiences, and save favorite businesses.
- **Local Content Feed:** A dynamic feed showcasing new businesses, events, promotions, and community news from local businesses.
- **Messaging (Potential for future iterations):** Direct communication channels between users and businesses.

## 5. Scope (Initial Phase)

- **In Scope:** Core functionalities for user and business profile creation, business discovery (search, browse), review/rating system, and basic content feed for one city.
- **Out of Scope (for initial release):** In-app purchasing, booking systems, complex loyalty programs, multi-city support, advanced analytics dashboards for businesses.

## 6. Success Metrics

- Number of active user profiles (monthly/weekly).
- Number of registered and active local business profiles.
- Average user engagement rate (e.g., number of searches, business profile views, reviews submitted).
- Business engagement (e.g., profile updates, number of promotions posted).
- User and business retention rates.
- Qualitative feedback from user and business surveys.
